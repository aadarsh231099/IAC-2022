Objectives


  1. To create an interface between Industries & Academia (Faculty, Researchers and Students) for interaction, transfer of knowledge, experience & technology.

  2. To understand the issues & challenges faced by the industries and to develop feasible & workable solutions.

  3. To bridge the gap between the needs of industries & expertise of academics.

  4. To showcase NMAMIT’s technological capabilities to create an environment for joint exploration and furthering of indigenous technologies, and to develop Centres of Excellence.

  5. To provide platform for joint innovative research involving experts from industry and academia.

Scope


  1. Create opportunities and exploit the available resources to benefit industry, academia and society.

  2. Foster collaborations for mutual benefits in the areas of research.

  3. Provide a common platform for the agglomeration of ideas of experts from various domains for learning enrichment.

  4. Create Centre of Excellence in niche areas of research.

TargetGroup

Outcome


  1. Maximize industrial connectivity.

  2. Interaction with industry expertise from various domains.

  3. Discussion with eminent persons from academia.

  4. Exploring internship and placement opportunities.

  5. Training in Industry, Consultancy, Projects in Industry.

  6. Enhancement of perception through joint projects and collaborations.
